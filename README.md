<h2>About The Project</h2>
Quentin Vocateur, a freelance demon summoning specialist, has to deal with eccentric clients and capricious demons. Between answering emails and urgent phone calls from his clients, he doesn't have a moment to himself. Unfortunately, this frantic pace leaves him no time to exorcise the demons he has summoned, so he must suffer the effects of his own invocations! Beware of burnout!
